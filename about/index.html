About Me

I am a second year studying cybersecurity 🤓 and just a year and a half didn’t have anything to do with IT, but the tables turned and now I am dying to get a deeper knowledge about most different topics from security to robotics. For some time, I’ve been an Arduino enthusiast, but also have bigger plans to get my hands dirty with Raspberry Pi and ESP32.

This blog was started after various of other blogs about IT I’ve read and wasn’t dissapointed, since most of the owners who wrote them were speaking from experience. No promises of quick learning, but actually suggesting books to get deeper into the topics.

I’m determined to get the most out of what I can read and find, so let’s do our best 🎉
